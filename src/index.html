About Us

It’s not every day that a company that delivers on creativity has scale. And it’s not every day that such a creative company with scale has a distinct culture and heart.

We, ELECTRIKFX, a service provider of Visual Effects for International projects and our services include Roto, Paint, Prep, Camera tracking and Rotomation.

We emerge with a vision to forge an organization where the great creative spirits and the pipeline developers work together to achieve your dreams by creating innovative digital imageries.

Our VFX team is of highly industrial experienced resources techno-creative experts, who worked on many biggest blockbusters and a young proficient group who is passionate about producing impressive imageries that express many exceptional stories.

We can handle projects of any scale & complexity and our dynamic resources provide a seamless workflow to ensure success in meeting deadlines and clients’ requirements. An expertise team is allocated for managing our well-organized Pipelines to make our work environment smoother and balance the work life of VFX artists within our studio.

More innovative ideas are in the development stage, and we are in the process of unlocking the maximum potential of business, yet there is no compromise on quality.

Our paint team is skilled in removing unwanted objects and elements from live-action footage, fixing continuity errors, and adding or removing textures. Our paint services are often used for compositing and clean-up work.

Our prep team is responsible for preparing footage for compositing. This involves cleaning up and enhancing the footage, including color correction, removing unwanted elements, and stabilizing footage.

Roto, short for rotoscoping, is the process of creating a matte or mask around an object in live-action footage. Our roto team is skilled in creating precise, detailed mattes to allow for accurate compositing of VFX elements into live-action footage.

Matchmoving involves tracking the movement of live-action footage to allow for the accurate placement of VFX elements. Our matchmove team is experienced in using both 2D and 3D tracking methods to ensure seamless integration of VFX into live-action footage.

Rotomation is the process of capturing the movement of a physical object and applying it to a digital model. Our rotomation team is skilled in capturing precise movement data, which can be used to create realistic animations of digital characters and objects.

Lord knows it’s been hard. At nearly 500 people strong and growing, we’re not doing victory laps, but instead doubling down on the leadership traits that helped us maintain this balance so far.
